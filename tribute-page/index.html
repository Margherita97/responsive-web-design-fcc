Dino Dvornik

This tribute page is dedicated to Dino Dvornik, also known as the king of Croatian funk.

Dino started his music career together with his brother, Dean, forming the duo "Kineski zid". After the release of their first, and only, album, also called Kineski zid, the band unfortunately split.

However, this didn't stop Dino, who went on releasing other 7 album which consacrated him as the King of Croatian Funk.
